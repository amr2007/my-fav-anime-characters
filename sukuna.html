Ryomen Sukuna

(Series:Jujutsu Kaisen)

Sukuna, known as the King of Curses, is the most feared entity in the Jujutsu Kaisen universe. Once a legendary sorcerer, his immense power was so overwhelming that even after death his soul lingered, split across twenty indestructible fingers. Revived inside Yuji Itadori’s body, Sukuna is cunning, cruel, and utterly ruthless — a being who treats human life as meaningless. Yet behind the malice, there’s a sharp intelligence and an almost playful arrogance that makes him terrifyingly charismatic. Every time he appears, he steals the spotlight, reminding both allies and enemies why he’s considered the ultimate curse.

8.5/10
