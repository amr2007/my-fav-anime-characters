Son Goku

(Series:Dragon Ball Z)

Goku, the cheerful Saiyan warrior, is the heart and soul of Dragon Ball Z. Raised on Earth but born from a warrior race, he combines boundless kindness with an unstoppable drive to grow stronger. Famous for his iconic Kamehameha and his ever-evolving transformations — from Super Saiyan to even godlike forms — Goku’s battles are legendary. Yet what makes him so beloved isn’t just his power, but his pure spirit: he treats every fight as a chance to push limits, protect his friends, and savor the thrill of combat. For fans, Goku is more than a hero — he’s the embodiment of perseverance and boundless energy.

8.8/10
