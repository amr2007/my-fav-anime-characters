Igris

(Series:Solo-Leveling)

Igris, the Blood-Red Commander, is one of the most iconic knights in Solo Leveling. At first glance, he looks like a towering suit of crimson armor brought to life, wielding a massive sword with frightening precision. Despite being born as a boss monster in a deadly dungeon, Igris stands out for his knightly aura — disciplined, loyal, and strangely honorable. After his unforgettable duel with Sung Jin-Woo, he doesn’t just fall as another defeated foe; instead, he rises again as one of Jin-Woo’s most trusted shadows. Silent but ever-present, Igris embodies the perfect mix of raw power and unwavering loyalty, making him one of the coolest companions in the series.

8.6/10
