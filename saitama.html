Saitama

(Series:One Punch Man)

Saitama, better known as One Punch Man, is the most overpowered hero in anime — and also the most hilariously bored. After years of “100 push-ups, 100 sit-ups, 100 squats, and a 10 km run every day,” he gained the ability to defeat any enemy with a single punch. Sounds amazing, right? Except there’s a catch: life becomes painfully dull when no fight lasts longer than three seconds. Beneath his plain yellow suit and blank expression, Saitama’s real battle isn’t against monsters or aliens — it’s against the crushing boredom of being invincible. That mix of absurd comedy and sly commentary on superhero tropes is what makes him unforgettable.

8.6/10
