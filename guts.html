Guts

(Series:Berserk)

Guts, known as the Black Swordsman, is a lone warrior forged in the fires of endless struggle. Wielding a colossal blade called the Dragonslayer, he carves his way through demons and destiny alike with raw determination. His life is marked by betrayal, tragedy, and relentless battles against forces far larger than himself, yet he endures with an unbreakable will. Beneath his scarred exterior lies a man searching for purpose, love, and freedom in a brutal world. Guts is both terrifyingly strong and profoundly human, making him one of the most unforgettable characters in dark fantasy.

8.7/10
